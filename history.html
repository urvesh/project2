Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs (February 24, 1955)
is an American marketer, and inventor, who is the co-founder and CEO of Apple Inc.
In April 1976 Jobs, along with Steve Wozniak form the Apple Computer Company.
With his insight and leadership, he has led to over 50,000 Apple II unit sales!

Stephen Gary "Steve" Wozniak (August 11, 1950)
is an American electrical engineer who co-founded Apple Computer Inc. Wozniak single-handedly designed both the Apple I and Apple II computers. With Wozniak's computing skills and innovative designs, he helped bring us the Apple II!

Apple Computer Inc. (established April 1, 1976)
was co-founded by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak.

highly successful Apple II, we at Apple want to assure you

products, as we have shown throughout our history!
